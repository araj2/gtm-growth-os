# Annual Planning Cycle Runbook

## Purpose

Program-manage the annual GTM planning cycle so targets, territories, capacity, comp, and systems land on time and reconcile to the company plan.

## Workstreams

- Finance plan
- Segment targets
- Sales capacity plan
- Territory design
- Quota deployment
- Compensation design
- Marketing plan
- BDR outbound plan
- Systems readiness
- Enablement rollout

## Control principles

- Quotas do not go out until territories are locked.
- Territories do not lock until account ownership exceptions are resolved.
- Comp plans do not launch until quotas reconcile to buffered plan.
- Systems readiness must be complete before field rollout.
- Enablement must include manager and rep-facing operating standards.

## Output artifacts

- Planning calendar
- DRI tracker
- Dependency map
- Executive decision log
- Final operating plan
